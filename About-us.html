Our Background

Discover how we have evolved our company’s legacy.

Founded as part of Applied Integrators, myQuickHR is a wholly Malaysian-owned company dedicated to providing innovative HR solutions. Our flagship platform, HR Avenue, streamlines human resources processes through automated workflows, notifications, reminders, and task management, increasing organizational efficiency.

Beyond automation, HR Avenue provides powerful decision-making support through dashboards, executive centers, and managerial self-service portals, all accessible to employees for a seamless HR experience.

With over a decade of experience, myQuickHR has successfully served clients across diverse industries including finance, government, services, property, automotive, and manufacturing, earning a reputation for reliability and innovation.
