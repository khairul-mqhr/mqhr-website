Organized by

Highlights
Innovation & Collaboration

MyQuickHR had the pleasure of participating in the SME Biz Day 2024, a prestigious event organized by CTOS in collaboration with our esteemed partner, AWS. This full-day event was not only fruitful but also a significant milestone for our company.

Our presence at the event provided a wonderful opportunity to collaborate with AWS and showcase our innovative HRMS solutions to a broader audience. The exposure was invaluable and opened doors for potential expansion in our client network.

We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to AWS for inviting us to collaborate and for their continuous support and partnership. The event’s success underscores the strength of our collaboration, and we look forward to many more opportunities to work together in the future.
